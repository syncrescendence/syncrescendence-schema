# Artifact Templates

Structural templates governing artifact format across all salons in the Syncrescendence constellation.

## Canonical reference

`ARTIFACT-TEMPLATES-VIZIER.md` is the canonical, surface-neutral specification. It defines the three artifact types (Q/A, Synthesis, Dispatch), the common header block, naming conventions, atrium promotion criteria, and dispatch protocol per target agent.

## Platform-adapted variants

- `ARTIFACT-TEMPLATES-VANGUARD.md` — Adapted for the Vanguard salon (ChatGPT / GPT-5.4 Canvas). Includes Canvas-specific output discipline, handoff optimization for clipboard relay, and the Vanguard's strategic-anchor role framing.
- `ARTIFACT-TEMPLATES-ORACLE.md` — Adapted for the Oracle salon (Grok 4.20 on grok.com / 4.1 Fast via API). Includes the dual-instrument distinction (sensing vs. decomposition), adversarial sensing protocol, evidence packet structure, and source manifest requirements.

## Relationship to grammar

These templates govern format. Content discipline, epistemic standards, and structural authority derive from the grammar layer at `grammar/`. The templates implement the grammar's requirements in surface-specific terms — they do not extend or override constitutional law.
