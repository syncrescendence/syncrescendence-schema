# Grammar-Aligned Research Methodology — CLI Supplementation Path

## Basis and boundary

This document stays inside the commission's scope. It designs a research methodology concrete enough that Commander can execute one full grammar-aligned cycle now, with the outputs serving as operational proof from which the first minimal contracts can later be extracted.

The grammar layer must move upstream and become the instrument that shapes what is observed, how it is labeled, how disagreement is preserved, how convergence is tested, and how scaffold-readiness is judged. Pre-grammar research produced rich narrative exegesis; Phase 2 reformatted that exegesis into grammar-compliant artifacts. The inversion starts here.

CLI supplementation solves the context-injection problem explicitly. The grammar layer can be loaded as direct working context into every research agent. The research method can demand axis-by-axis decomposition, primitive-floor extraction, epistemic labeling, source-granular evidence, staleness anchoring, and comparison-ready output in one pass. The result is an evidence packet already shaped for scaffold production, disagreement analysis, and receipt emission.

The methodology treats grammar-aligned research as a five-artifact loop: commission, evidence packet, disagreement map, synthesis output, and invocation receipt. That is the smallest loop that reduces the research debt and produces the operational traffic Lane B needs.

## I. The governing architecture of the method

The method operates as a three-pass refinery.

**Pass one — Decomposition.** Each agent receives the same target surface, the same modality-class axis set, the same epistemic protocol, the same output schema, and the same boundary constraints. The task: produce an axis-bounded evidence packet — what primitives exist under each axis, what evidence supports them, what their epistemic status is, what remains thin, and what cannot yet be resolved.

**Pass two — Disagreement extraction.** The agents' outputs are compared to surface contradictions, omissions, boundary disagreements, and granularity disagreements. Three model outputs become useful when forced into collision. This is where the methodological gain lives.

**Pass three — Bounded synthesis.** Commander (or whichever synthesis surface is active) begins from the compared evidence packets and the disagreement map. It constructs either a scaffold-ready decomposition, a calibration delta against an existing scaffold, or a research insufficiency report explaining why scaffold production remains premature.

This architecture preserves the distinction between observation, inference, contradiction, and synthesis — the same distinction the grammar already requires for scaffolds and methods. The research method mirrors the constitutional shape it serves.

## II. The research prompt architecture

The prompt has one invariant spine across all agents. The grammar supplies the ontology.

**Ratified frame.** The modality class, the surface under study, the relevant grammar documents, the closed axis set, and the output species being targeted. The agent receives explicit declaration of whether it is producing input toward a surface scaffold, a layer scaffold, a cross-cutting scaffold, or a calibration packet. This declaration governs evidence quality because the agent knows what structural floor it is reaching for.

**Scope law.** What is in bounds and out of bounds. For a reasoning/chat surface, in-bounds means context-window architecture, steering surface, cognitive signature and failure modes, output scaling behavior, tool-use capabilities, inter-model handoff contract, and platform constraint surface. Out of bounds means teleological recommendation, comparative ranking, institutional role assignment, or implementation planning. Descriptive purity from the start.

**Axis charter.** Each axis stated as a fixed heading with a one-line clarification of what counts as evidence under that axis. This disambiguates interpretation: "Steering surface" could mean custom instructions only, or also hidden memory and platform defaults. "Output scaling behavior" could mean verbosity patterns, reasoning-depth changes, or mode-based expansion. The clarifications make outputs comparable.

**Primitive-floor instruction.** The agent identifies structural elements at the lowest meaningful floor for each axis: distinct enough to matter downstream, granular enough to avoid collapsing into implementation dust. The grammar's granularity check made operational.

**Epistemic protocol.** Every claim labeled as verified (grounded in primary docs or direct observation), inferred (supported by patterns or indirect evidence), or provisional (plausible but thin). Unlabeled substantive claims are prohibited. This makes later synthesis able to distinguish hard substrate from interpretive speculation without rereading the whole source base.

**Evidence anchoring.** Every primitive candidate carries source references, observation date where possible, and an evidence-thinness marker. A model that declares "I lack sufficient evidence here" performs institutional work. A model that fills the gap with confident prose produces waste.

**Negative obligations.** The agent must preserve axis boundaries (no merging), maintain descriptive stance (no recommendations), preserve contradictions between sources (no smoothing), and maintain primitive distinctness (no collapsing distinct elements into convenience phrases without explicit structural justification).

**Output schema.** Rigid enough for mechanical comparison across three agents. Template: header, scope statement, axis-by-axis decomposition, primitive table per axis, unresolved questions per axis, contradiction notes, and end-state judgment using one of four labels: scaffold-ready, scaffold-ready-with-thin-zones, insufficient-evidence, or axis-conflict-escalation.

### Signature adaptation

The invariant spine stays constant across Commander, Adjudicator, Cartographer, and Oracle. Each receives a variant emphasis.

Commander receives the execution-oriented version: strict schema adherence, source discipline, explicit flags for where the packet would fail scaffold validation.

Adjudicator receives the forensic variant: aggressively tests granularity, internal consistency, missing axes, unsupported claims, and bad identifier candidates. Rigor over richness.

Cartographer receives the homology variant: attention to whether the target surface expresses primitives structurally analogous to already-known surfaces, and where it departs in ways that may matter for class-level convergence.

Oracle receives the adversarial variant: contradictions, edge-case evidence, atypical reports, failure modes, and evidence against the default interpretation. Oracle destabilizes premature certainty.

Scope law, axis set, epistemic protocol, and output schema stay invariant. Comparability depends on it.

## III. The calibration protocol

The first cycle targets a surface with rich pre-grammar scaffold lineage. Slack is the strongest candidate — widest delta signal. The comparison uses a fixed calibration matrix across five dimensions.

**Axis coverage.** Did the grammar-aligned packet populate every declared axis? Did the pre-grammar artifact cover the same ground in substance, even if differently structured? Missing-axis rate is the fastest test for hidden blind spots.

**Primitive yield by axis.** Structurally distinct primitives identified per axis in the grammar-aligned packet versus the existing scaffold. Higher yield is informative when it reveals where the old artifact was sparse because the research never looked there.

**Epistemic distribution.** What proportion of primitives are verified, inferred, or provisional in the new packet versus the existing artifact once retrospectively labeled? A pre-grammar scaffold may appear complete while resting on a large inferred core. This dimension exposes that gap.

**Thin-zone topology.** Where is evidence thin — and does thinness cluster? Systematic thinness in one axis across multiple calibration surfaces signals that the methodology, the available sources, or the axis interpretation needs correction.

**Prescriptive contamination and traceability.** How much material in the pre-grammar artifact was teleological, comparative, or evaluative rather than descriptive? How addressable are the primitives for downstream reuse? This reveals whether the old artifact was structurally serviceable or merely eloquent.

### Calibration judgment bands

**Mostly adequate:** Axis coverage near-complete, primitive yield differences modest, thin zones distributed without pathological clustering.

**Adequate but biased:** Old scaffold captured much of the class while systematically underpopulating specific axes or masking inference density.

**Systematically blind:** Multiple axes weak, primitive yield differences large, epistemic discipline poor, or prescriptive content distorting the descriptive substrate.

### Calibration sample size

One surface proves executability. Three calibration surfaces prove reliability. Choose for structural diversity within the class: for SaaS, one communication-heavy surface, one structured-work surface, one block/object-model surface. For reasoning/chat, one heavy-tool-integration model, one strong-conversational-identity model, one constrained-surface-behavior model. The method must survive different architectural temperaments.

## IV. The convergence methodology

The convergence question: when has the convergent primitive core stabilized enough that additional sampling produces mainly local divergence rather than class-defining structure?

A sequential saturation method. Begin with a seed sample spanning the obvious subfamilies of the modality class. Build scaffold-ready packets for that seed. Extract provisional convergent primitives from comparison. Then add one new surface at a time using deliberate diversity sampling: pick the next surface because it is structurally likely to challenge the current core.

After each addition, measure four things: new convergent primitives introduced, existing convergent primitives invalidated or split, major boundary reclassifications, and net change in class synthesis observations. Additive novelty and reorganization both matter. A new surface that forces you to split a previously convergent primitive carries more information than one that adds an idiosyncratic flourish.

**Stopping rule:** Continue sampling until, across three consecutive additions, no new convergent primitives are introduced, no existing convergent primitive is split or reclassified, and all novelty absorbs into divergent or local forms. This proves provisional class stability.

**Supporting ratios.** Core stability ratio: convergent primitives unchanged divided by total convergent primitives. Novelty absorption ratio: new observations landing in divergence or local specialization divided by all new observations. When core stability stays high and novelty absorption rises over consecutive additions, the class is stabilizing. This approach recognizes reclassification as important signal — the question includes "did something I thought was universal turn out to be provincial?"

## V. The disagreement extraction protocol

Three decompositions of the same surface are compared through a disagreement grid, axis-first. For each axis, compare agents across four dimensions: primitive presence or absence, primitive boundary definition, epistemic status assignment, and source support. The matrix makes disagreement visible as structure.

Four disagreement types matter:

**Omission disagreement:** One agent finds a primitive and another does not. Signals source-coverage differences, model blindness, or genuine ambiguity about structural salience.

**Boundary disagreement:** Agents identify the same general element but partition it differently. One agent sees one primitive where another sees three. The most important type — directly bears on primitive-floor correctness.

**Classification disagreement:** Agents place the same primitive under different axes or assign different epistemic statuses. Often reveals axis ambiguity or unstable grammar interpretation.

**Confidence disagreement:** Agents agree a primitive exists but disagree on verified, inferred, or provisional status. Tests evidence sufficiency.

### Adjudication hierarchy

Prefer primary-source support over secondary pattern recognition. Prefer more sharply bounded primitives over vaguer umbrellas when evidence warrants the split. Prefer preserved ambiguity over forced resolution when the source base remains thin. Preserve unresolved disagreement explicitly when neither side can be cleanly defeated.

### Noise versus signal

Model noise is distinguished from genuine disagreement by repetition and grounding. An anomaly appearing in only one agent, lacking clear source support, and absent across calibration surfaces is likely noise. Disagreement recurring across agents or surfaces, or clustering around a particular axis, reveals methodological or domain ambiguity worth preserving.

The synthesis agent asks: what kind of disagreement is this, what evidence class supports each side, and does this disagreement expose a weakness in the method, the axis definition, the source base, or the surface itself?

## VI. The first-cycle operating sequence

Commission the target surface and species. Load the grammar layer excerpts actually needed — the modality-class axis set, scaffold validation criteria, epistemic protocol, and output schema.

Run the decomposition pass in parallel across Adjudicator, Cartographer, and Oracle. Keep Commander out of first-pass generation so Commander remains available as bounded synthesist and validator.

Normalize the three evidence packets into the same comparison format. Generate the disagreement grid.

Commander produces three outputs from the combined packet: a scaffold-ready decomposition if warranted, a calibration delta against the existing scaffold if one exists, and an invocation receipt recording what ran, what evidence classes were used, what the major disagreements were, and the resulting readiness judgment.

That receipt is the fossil ancestor of the formal Tier 3 contract. Operational contracts emerge from observed traffic patterns; they are extracted, not prescribed.

Update present state only after the receipt exists. Research work emits governed state changes, not just files.

## VII. The skill encoding

The skill encodes the complete bounded operation.

**Inputs:** Target surface or class, scaffold species target, modality-class axis set, research scope statement, evidence sources or source-access plan, comparison mode flag (fresh scaffold, calibration, convergence sample, disagreement review), and operator-set constraints (time budget, excluded source types).

**Internal stages:** Stage one loads constitutional context and validates axis set declaration. Stage two generates or ingests agent packets. Stage three normalizes and compares. Stage four produces scaffold-ready output or insufficiency report. Stage five emits invocation receipt and state-update recommendations.

**Outputs (typed at file level):** `RESEARCH-COMMISSION`, `EVIDENCE-PACKET`, `DISAGREEMENT-MAP`, `CALIBRATION-DELTA` or `SCAFFOLD-DRAFT`, and `INVOCATION-RECEIPT`. File-level typing gives operational repeatability now and makes later contract extraction easier.

**Validation criteria:** Axis completeness, epistemic-label completeness, source traceability, explicit thin-zone marking, disagreement classification present when multi-agent mode is used, and final readiness judgment declared. For scaffold-ready outputs, preflight against the twelve checks.

**Named failure modes:** Axis ambiguity, primitive overcompression, source starvation, adversarial drift into teleology, false convergence across agents, explosion of unresolved disagreements, context overload from excessive grammar injection. Each failure produces a bounded failure artifact, not silence.

The operator provides the research brief: target, scope, species, purpose, and live constraints. The skill provides the decomposition frame, epistemic law, comparison machinery, output schema, and receipt discipline. The grammar carries the context. The operator stops hand-carrying hidden knowledge.

## VIII. Directional conclusion

Adopt the methodology in narrow, empirical form first. The first objective: prove that grammar-injected CLI research produces evidence packets more structurally useful than pre-grammar exegesis, while also emitting the comparison artifacts and receipts that operational contracts will later formalize.

Success yields three things simultaneously. It pays down the research debt. It generates real traffic for Lane B contract extraction. And it reveals, in bounded form, how each avatar's cognitive signature contributes to decomposition, adversarial challenge, comparison, and synthesis.

This is the right next move because it manufactures the first lawful operational bones from work that the institution must do anyway.
